








# Shubh Connections

 Shubh Connection is a site that hopes to help keep people motivated to meet up for yoga on a regular basis in Navan co Meath, Ireland. The site will be targeted toward yoga enthusiasts who are looking for a way to socialise and keep themselves fit. Shubh  Connections will be useful for users to see exactly when and where they should be to join the Shubh Connections courses and events. The page gives an overview about the studio and its history.The page outlines the services that be booked at the studio. Shubh Connections yoga studio wants to help the user achieve optimum health and achieve their well being  and stress release goals.

![Responsice Mockup](https://github.com/Tinks18/Yoga-project/blob/main/docs/sconnections_mock-up.jpg)


## The site structure:

The site has four links on the landing page that open different sections on the same page that can also be seen by scrolling down.
The four sections are:
- About Us
- Gallery 
- Meet the Teacher
- Contact Us

## Testing 

Here is a list of features of the website that were tested and the tests and list of the tests that were performed after which it was concluded that the site legitimately  works well. 

- The  Navigation bar has four links that when clicked take you different sections of the website. 
- The Navigation bar was fixed to the top so that the diffrent sections and links get highlighted when the user scrolls the website.
- The navigation bar gets converted in to a burger menu on mobile screens which when clicked gives a drop down menu of the links.
- The about us section is put on transparent background and text is coloured white thus giving a high contrast for ease of reading.
- The about us section is responsive on different tablets and mobile screens.
- The gallery section is responsive as the pictures stack on top of each other on mobile screens and tablet screens.
- The pictures can be scrolled individually and zoomed into on all devices.
- The Meet the teacher section panel opens and closes within the background and is responsive.
- The testimonials are responsive too and stack on top of each other on mobile screens and tablets when for smaller screen sizes.
- The Contact me form is responsive and adjusts to screen sizes where the form stacks under the address section.
- The map is responsive to diffrent screen sizes and pans left and right on touch to show locations.
- The social links open onto  different page when clicked.

Over all the testing looks like all the features are working as intended and are easy and straightforward for the users.
