Established in 2016 to cater to the need for wellness for the community. The Shubh-Connections Centre is located in the serene countryside. Boyne Valley is a popular tourist attraction. Its proximity to Trim Castle and River Boyne makes it the perfect setting to unite Mind, Body & Soul.
The Centre provides:

  • MBSR eight week course
    (Mindfulness-based stress reduction)
  • Mindful eating course
  • Yoga classes
  • Meditation sessions
  • Day or Weekend Yoga Retreats
