About Us

Established in 2016 to cater to the need for wellness for the community. The Shubh-Connections Centre is located in the serene countryside. Boyne Valley is a popular tourist attraction. Its proximity to Trim Castle and River Boyne makes it the perfect setting to unite Mind, Body & Soul.

Courses :

  • MBSR eight week course
    (Mindfulness-based stress reduction)
  • Mindful eating course
  • Yoga classes
  • Meditation sessions
  • Day or Weekend Yoga Retreats

About Us


Aparna is a certified Mindfulness, MBSR & Yoga Teacher & a Qualified Nurse

Read More

She started practising Raj Yoga in 2012 and subsequently became a Yoga Alliance certified Yoga Teacher. She studied "Therapeutic Use of Mindfulness" in 2015 from the Institute of Integrative Counselling and Psychotherapy in Ireland. In 2016, Aparna studied Reflective Practice at University College Dublin. Aparna has completed MBSR Teacher training from Mindful Academy Solterreno from Spain and yoga philosophy teacher training with the Eire Vedanta Society. She continues to learn mindfulness with various courses such as " Self Compassion" & "Working Well Mindfully".

Aparna regularly facilitates Mindfulness & Meditation workshops for the Irish Nurses and Midwives Organisation and HSE Ireland.As a Mindfulness teacher, she held workshops for IT Professionals and Industrial Relation Officers, Physiotherapists, Health Care Assistants, Elderly Care Workers and PhD students at the University.

She spoke in many International Webinars on Yoga & Mindfulness to support people in their wellness journey.
